HOME TRIMWORK​

Trimwork, the distinctive architectural details that adorn the finest of homes, gives any bland room charm and personality. No other type of home décor, furnishings, wallcoverings or artwork can finish off a room as effectively as trimwork. Different types of decorative mouldings create various styles, depending upon your home’s décor. The variety is wonderful – custom crown moulding, decorative beams, baseboards, wainscoting, chair rails, cornices, door and window casings and more. And whether you prefer a contemporary or traditional look, subtle elegance or a more opulent feel, a professional trimwork carpenter will discover the decorative trim that’s right for you.

Our experts make:

  • MOLDINGS
    • Crown molding, Chair molding, Wainscot, Wall panels, pillars, mantles, Door Ways, Window molding, ornamental styles available, and more…
  • BUILT-INS
    • Bookshelves & Built-Ins, Office Cabinets, Window Seats, Mudroom cubes, Entertainment centers, and more…
  • STAIR WORK
    • Iron Balusters, wood balusters, Step refinishing, hand rails and newels, curved stairs
  • PAINTING
    • Painting of walls and ceiling painting, drywall repairs, Painting of moldings.
